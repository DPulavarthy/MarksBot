Information Collection and Use

Your USER ID may be stored on our database if you use certain commands that can log high scores or if you use commands such as the todo lists and reminders - Your GUILD ID is also collected for statistical purposes. You can delete all of your information without having to request us to delete it by doing m.delete allmydata this will delete all your USER data (high scores, reminders, todo lists etc) and if you want to remove all the data we have on the server (warnings, word filter preferences, starboard data etc) then you can run m.delete serverdata

Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you visit our Service, we collect information that your browser sends to us that is called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your computer’s Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser version, pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other statistics. If you submit information to us on any of the forms we may also log your IP address to prevent abuse.

Auto Moderation

Filtered messages m.filter will get logged to the server's set log channel (if one is set) If you don't want your messages to be logged to the server's log channel then please leave that server, message filtering is disabled by default.
